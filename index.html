  • It's a Minefield Out There: The game board is a grid full of cells. Some cells have bombs that will blast you to smithereens! Other cells are safe and cozy.
  • Navigate: You can rotate, zoom, and pan around the board using your mouse for a better view of the minefield.
  • Choose Wisely Grasshopper: Click a cell to reveal it. If it's safe, you earn points. If it's a bomb, you lose a life.
  • Mark Suspicious Cells: Right-click a cell to place a flag if you think it's a bomb. This helps you remember dangerous spots.
  • Mission: Don't Explode! Reveal enough safe cells to reach the winning score while steering clear of bombs to win.
  • Difficulty Modes: Choose your challenge level:
    • Easy: Fewer bombs (10-15) and more lives (3)
    • Medium: Moderate bombs (15-25) and standard lives (3)
    • Hard: Many bombs (25-35) and fewer lives (2)

Enjoy the immersive experience with realistic graphics, explosion effects and dynamic lighting!
