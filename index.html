How to Play 3D Minesweeper

  • It's a Minefield Out There: The 3D game board is a grid full of cells. Some cells have bombs that will blast you to smithereens! Other cells are safe and cozy.
  • Navigate in 3D: You can rotate, zoom, and pan around the board using your mouse for a better view of the minefield.
  • Choose Wisely Grasshopper: Click a cell to reveal it. If it's safe, you earn points. If it's a bomb, you lose a life.
  • Mark Suspicious Cells: Right-click a cell to place a flag if you think it's a bomb. This helps you remember dangerous spots.
  • Mission: Don't Explode! Reveal enough safe cells to reach the winning score while steering clear of bombs to win.

Enjoy the immersive 3D experience with explosion effects and realistic interactions!
